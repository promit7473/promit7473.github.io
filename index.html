> About Me

As a passionate Robotics and Mechatronics Engineering student at the University of Dhaka, I'm driven by the intersection of hardware and intelligent software, aiming to create autonomous systems that interact with the physical world. My primary focus is mobile robotics, where I'm developing skills in ROS2, Gazebo, and relevant programming languages, designing algorithms for navigation, perception, and control. I'm also actively researching Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) within ROS2, utilizing platforms like Isaac Sim, and am a strong advocate for collaborative learning and open-source contributions.
